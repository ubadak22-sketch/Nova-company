About

NOVA is an independent, early-stage research initiative—not yet a registered legal entity. We are a small team with a long-term vision, operating with transparency about our current status and future intent.

Legal Status: This initiative is currently operating as an independent research project. We are not yet incorporated as a legal entity and do not currently offer commercial services or products.

Our focus is on foundational thinking around technology, systems design, and artificial intelligence. We believe in slow, careful research that prioritizes clarity and intellectual integrity over rapid scaling or market claims.

We operate with a non-profit mindset—our goal is to contribute meaningfully to human knowledge and capability, not to extract value. We are transparent about our limitations, uncertainties, and current phase of development.

If you are interested in collaborating, discussing research directions, or following our work, we would welcome conversation.

Research Focus

AI & Systems Research

We explore foundational questions about artificial intelligence, alignment, and systems behavior. Our approach emphasizes first-principles thinking, careful analysis of emergent behaviors, and long-term implications of technology development.

Software & Infrastructure

We investigate how systems are built, how they scale, and how design decisions ripple through technical and social ecosystems. We focus on clarity, maintainability, and the relationship between code and consequence.

Long-Term Technology Strategy

We think about what sustainable, responsible technology development looks like over decades. This includes questions of governance, safety, coordination, and how technology can support human flourishing rather than replace human agency.
